# Scoring Rubric

## Categories (1-5 each)

- Problem framing
- Requirement clarification
- Capacity estimation accuracy
- Architecture clarity
- Data/API depth
- Scalability and reliability reasoning
- Tradeoff quality
- Communication clarity

## Signals for Strong Performance

- Quantifies assumptions and revises when challenged.
- Explains why one design is chosen over alternatives.
- Handles failure scenarios without hand-waving.
- Keeps user impact and operational cost visible.
